TANDOORI ROTI

Quick Check

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Resting Time: 1 hour

Cook Time: 10 minutes

Total Time: 1 hour 20 minutes

Serves: 4 people

INGREDIENTS
Item Quantity
Whole Wheat Flour 2.5 cups
Sugar 1 teaspoon
Baking Powder 1 teaspoon
Baking Soda 0.25 teaspoon
Salt 0.5 teaspoon
Curd 0.5 cup
Water For kneading, as per requirement
Oil 2 tablespoon
PROCEDURE
  1. Take 2.5 cup wheat flour, 1 teaspoon sugar, 1 teaspoon baking powder, 0.25 teaspoon baking soda and 0.5 teaspoon salt in a large bowl.
  2. Mix well, making sure everything is well combined.
  3. Now add 0.5 cup curd and start kneading the dough.
  4. Add water as required and continue to knead for 5 minutes.
  5. Now add 2 tablespoon oil and knead until the dough turns smooth and soft.
  6. Cover the dough well, and let it rest for 1 hour or more.
  7. After 1 hour, knead the dough again, making sure that it is soft.
  8. Pinch a ball-sized dough and dust it with wheat flour.
  9. Roll the dough ball gently, making sure the roti is slightly thick. Sprinkle some water over it and spread uniformly.
  10. Heat a pan on medium flame, and once it is hot, transfer the roti onto the tawa. Make sure that the waterside is facing down. Press gently, so that the roti sticks to the pan.
  11. Cook the roti for a minute, or until the roti partially puffs up and the base gets cooked up.
  12. Now flip the pan onto the flame and cook until the roti is cooked uniformly. Do not worry if there are some black spots appearing: it gives a "tandoor effect".
  13. Now scrape the roti from the surface of the pan, and make sure that it is cooked completely from all the sides.
  14. Finally, brush the tandoori roti with butter (optional). Serve hot with sabzi/dal.
